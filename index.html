Ring the doorbell!

A unique experience. You must go inside and fight your own fears. If you don’t survive, authentic malefic creatures will kill you. Show your courage and try to escape alive and safe.

Characters


Chuky

Small but intrepid with his sharp knife is waiting to kill you. On his face some scars. He looks like a child, but he's a serial killer. You must be agile and quick to escape from him.

Freddy

A crazy old man with a disfigured face, an old hat, a striped sweater and gloves with very sharp and bloody blades made by himself. When he unleashes his rage it becomes a killing machine.

Jason

Completely crazy. Armed with sharp machete of large measures. No mercy to kill you. He has a supernatural strength and will unleash his rage attacks against you
